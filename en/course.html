Introduction

Teaching by full-time teachers such as the Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Dept. of Computer Science and Information Engineering, Dept. of Mechanical and Electro-Mechanical Engineering and Dept. of Architecture.

Courses

  1. The purpose of the courses is to teach and train students so that students have the ability to research and integrate professional knowledge.
  2. Student must take 24 credits in the courses, and provide doctoral thesis. The student will receive a doctorate after oral defense.
